Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g of pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of cherry tomatoes
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Boil the pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Step 2: Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© the garlic.
  3. Step 3: Add cherry tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Step 4: Drain the pasta and toss it with the tomato mixture.
  5. Step 5: Garnish with fresh basil and serve warm.
